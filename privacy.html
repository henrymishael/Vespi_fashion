    This document establishes the privacy policies of Vespi, its websites as well as as its compliance with specific national jurisdictions (including the privacy regulations and laws thereof). The publication of this document rescinds all previous privacy policies - implied, spoken, or written.

  • Organization Information The principal activities of Vespi are fashion display and advertisement. Modern fashion information technologies play a fundamental role in the activities of Vespi.

  • Anonymous Access Visitors can access the website/application home pages and browse sites/applications without disclosing personal data.

  • Data Collection and Purpose

    We may use the personal data supplied or provided by you to send you emails and/or push notifications containing invitations for participations in promotions. Your participation is completely voluntary and you can opt-out at any time of promotional emails because Vespi employs a strict email policy against unsolicited emails. You may opt out by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of every email. Please note that opting out from promotional emails does not exempt you from receiving administrative emails about your status changes.

  • Disclosure

    Except as set forth herein, Vespi does not disclose personal data to subsidiaries or other orgainizations without your permission or where it can reasonably be inferred from the circumstances that you consent to the disclosure to the third parties. Vespi tries to limit the information it provides to third parties to the information they need to help Vespi provide services to you. Vespi may not share non-personally identifiable information collected via the service in aggregate, anonymlus form with advertisers or other third parties so that they may better evaluate what services are most appealing to different segments of the user base. Vespi does not disclose your first name/last name, address, email address or any other personal information to these third parties unless you give your express consent. ..
